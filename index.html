Living Haitian heroes of today

When we think about heroes, people tend to look toward the past, our glorious age. But what if we can be living heroes? The brave souls who saved Cap-Haitien from pollution and insalubrity. The untiring women and men who leave a healthy city to the next generation?

For some years now, the city of Cap-Haitien has been reputed for being one of the dirtiest city in the country. Where ever you go, you encounter a pile of garbage, if not many next to each other. They stink and sometimes they hinders the traffic. The bad management of garbage in Cap-Haitien puts the health of the population in danger. The citizens often extinguish the garbage in the street. That makes the situation worse. When we look closer to the composition of the trashes, we notice that 70% of it are plastic bottles, carriers, cups that people usually buy food in.

So what can we do as living heroes of today?

Cap-heroes wants to be the answer. We plan to raise awareness about the pollution and the insalubrity that is plaguing Cap-Haitien. We want to educate the community to think differently when dealing with trashes, and to reduce the amount of waste they are producing. We want to discuss with the big companies which produce plastic bottles to make effective recycling. We developed the collection as an effective way to make re-using possible. Our enterprise covers the 4 Rs for a better environment: Re-think, Reduce, Reuse and Recycle.

What do we do?

Cap-Heroes is a social enterprise who sells reusable and portable eating kits. We currently have Three collections : Starter, Schooler and Traveller. Our primary focus is our community, Cap-Haitien. We are advocating for a clean, green and safe city. Our collections can help to reduce the amount of plastic waste both on the land and on the ocean. However to reach our goal, more work need to be done. That's why our team believe in :

Local Project

20% of our profit go back to our community. We support and invest in local project who aim to improve the environmental quality of our community.

Partnership

We work with leaders and enterprises to to create a clean and safe place to live.

Education

We believe in education. We provide free learning ressources about garbage sort and garbage management.
