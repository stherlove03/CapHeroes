What People say about us

Our Team

Esther-Love Prisnie Desravines, CEO

Esther is a young social entrepreneur passionate about technology. She’s a junior in High school and the founder of CM2Tech, an organization that organizes extracurricular activities focused on Science, technology, culture and leadership to help other students in the process of choosing their careers. Esther is a true citizen of Cap-Haitien. Her dream is to contribute in making her birth city a reference in Haiti, like king Henri 1st did in the past. She is the founder and the CEO of Cap-Heroes. Her goal is to give an alternative to the use of plastic and promote a clean, green and safe city.

Lyne Prisca Desravines, Sales and marketing manager

Lyne is a successful entrepreneur and the co-founder of Fantasia Event, an enterprise that organizes events, design and prepare gift baskets and decoration.
Lyne has a medical degree and advocates for a better healthcare in Haiti. For the last five years, she has been working in branding, sales and marketing.

She worked as community manager for two years for many big companies before working as the head of marketing for Cap-Heroes.

Ruth Prime Flore Desravines, Manager of operation

Prime-Flore majored in Tourism and hospitality with a track in Marketing, and she has an MBA in international business. She speaks five languages: Creole, French, English, Spanish and German. For the past three years, she has been working essentially with startups. She advocates against marine pollution and other environmental issues. She’s in charge of our operations, from stock management to vendors relations.
